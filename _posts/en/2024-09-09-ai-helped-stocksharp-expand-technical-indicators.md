The [StockSharp](https://stocksharp.ru/topic/26179/chto-novogo-i-kak-ii-pomog-nam-uvelichit-bazu-tehnicheskih-indikatorov/) team shared how they managed to significantly expand their library of technical indicators using artificial intelligence. Thanks to AI, the process of adding and optimizing indicators became more efficient, allowing them to increase both the quantity and variety of available indicators.

AI analyzes existing indicators, identifies missing elements, and helps create new ones, simplifying the development and testing of strategies. This is especially useful for those who actively use technical analysis in their trading systems, as the expanded indicator library enables deeper and more precise market analysis.

If you're interested in technical analysis and want to learn more about new opportunities for strategy development, this material from StockSharp will be valuable!
