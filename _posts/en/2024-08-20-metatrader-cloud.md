Hey, friends!

Recently, [MetaTrader 5](https://www.metatrader5.com/ru/news/2360) launched a new cloud hosting service for traders that allows running trading robots and copying trades directly in the cloud. This solution is perfect for those who don't want to depend on local servers or computers and strive to ensure their trading strategies run stably 24/7.

The cloud hosting from MetaTrader 5 also ensures high reliability, reducing the risk of disconnections or technical failures, which is especially important for automated trading. Traders don't need to worry about keeping their devices running or monitoring internet connection stability -- the MetaTrader cloud takes care of all of that.

Interestingly, there are other cloud solutions for trading on the market. For example, [StockSharp](https://stocksharp.ru/products/%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%BB%D0%B0%D0%BA%D0%BE/) offers a similar service. It would be interesting to know if anyone has compared these two systems and how they stack up against each other in terms of capabilities.
