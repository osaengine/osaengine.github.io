StockSharp has announced the addition of new connectors, expanding the capabilities of its platform for traders.

## New Connectors

- **Alpaca**: Integration with Alpaca is now available, allowing traders to use this platform for stock trading.
- **Bybit**: Bybit support opens access to one of the leading cryptocurrency exchanges, providing new opportunities for crypto trading.
- **Tinkoff**: Integration with Tinkoff expands opportunities for trading Russian stocks and other financial instruments.
- **Polygon.io**: The new Polygon.io connector provides access to an extensive market data database and enhances analysis and trading strategies.

These updates are aimed at improving the functionality of the StockSharp platform, giving traders more tools and opportunities for successful trading.
