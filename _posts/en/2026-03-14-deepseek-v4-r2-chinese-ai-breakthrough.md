## DeepSeek: From Underdog to Leader

The Chinese company **DeepSeek** has made one of the most impressive leaps in AI history over the past year. DeepSeek V3, released in late 2025, stunned the industry with its price-to-quality ratio, and **DeepSeek R1** became the first truly competitive open-weight reasoning model.

Now on the horizon are **DeepSeek V4** and **R2**, with releases expected in the coming weeks.

## What We Know About DeepSeek R2

### Multimodal Reasoning

The main innovation of R2 is the ability to reason not only about text but also about:

- **Images** — charts, diagrams, screenshots
- **Tables** — financial data, statistics
- **Code** — visual debugging and architectural analysis

This makes the model significantly more useful for financial analysis, where charts and tables are the primary data format.

### Open Weights

DeepSeek continues its **open-weight** strategy: models will be available for download and local deployment. This is critically important for:

- **Hedge funds** that cannot send data to the cloud
- **Individual traders** who want to control their infrastructure
- **Researchers** who need access to weights for fine-tuning

### Expected Benchmarks

Significant improvements on major benchmarks (MMLU, MATH-500, SWE-Bench) compared to R1 are expected, though specific numbers have not yet been confirmed.

## DeepSeek V4: Next-Generation Base Model

If R2 is a reasoning model (analogous to o3 from OpenAI), then V4 is a base model (analogous to GPT-5). Expected features:

- **MoE architecture** (Mixture of Experts) with even greater efficiency
- **Expanded context window** up to 256K tokens
- **Improved instruction following** in Chinese and English
- **30-40% reduction in inference costs** compared to V3

## Why China Is Catching Up

Despite NVIDIA chip export restrictions, DeepSeek and other Chinese companies are finding ways to stay competitive:

1. **Algorithmic efficiency** — DeepSeek spends 10x less on training comparable models
2. **Architectural innovations** — Multi-head Latent Attention and other original solutions
3. **Scale of investment** — the Chinese government actively funds AI research
4. **Data access** — the enormous domestic market provides unique datasets

## What This Means for Traders

DeepSeek V4 and R2 with open weights represent an opportunity to:

- Run a powerful AI model **on your own servers** without depending on foreign clouds
- Bypass **sanctions restrictions** on access to Western company APIs
- Get a **free** GPT-5-level tool for market analysis
