AI has already proven its effectiveness in algorithmic trading. Here are several successful examples of its application:

## Examples:

1. **Price forecasting:**
   - Using neural networks to analyze time series and identify trends.

2. **News analysis:**
   - Automatically identifying events that may affect the market and adapting the strategy in real time.

3. **Arbitrage strategies:**
   - Identifying price discrepancies between markets using AI algorithms.

4. **Adaptive strategies:**
   - Models that change their behavior depending on current market conditions.

AI is becoming an increasingly popular tool in algorithmic trading thanks to its ability to analyze large volumes of data and make accurate decisions.
