Successful training of an AI robot requires high-quality and diverse data. This data helps the algorithm identify patterns and adapt to different market conditions.

## Main types of data:

1. **Market data:**
   - Historical quotes (open, close, high, low prices).
   - Trading volumes.
   - Order book data and market depth.

2. **Fundamental data:**
   - Company financial reports.
   - Economic indicators (GDP, inflation, interest rates).

3. **Event data:**
   - News, press releases, and company reports.
   - Impact of macroeconomic events (central bank meetings, sanctions).

4. **Alternative data:**
   - Social media and forum data.
   - Sentiment analysis of texts.

High-quality data is the foundation for successful AI training and the creation of effective trading strategies.
