Artificial intelligence (AI) in trading robots is a technology that allows algorithms to analyze data, make decisions, and adapt to market changes. The core principle of AI is to simulate human analytical methods, but with greater speed and scale.

## Key elements of AI:

1. **Machine learning (ML):**
   - Algorithms that learn from data to identify patterns.
   - Used for price forecasting, trend detection, and market signal analysis.

2. **Neural networks:**
   - Models inspired by the human brain that can process complex multidimensional data.
   - Applied for time series analysis, pattern recognition, and automated decision-making.

3. **Natural language processing (NLP):**
   - Technologies for analyzing textual data such as news, articles, and social media posts.
   - Helps assess the impact of news on the market.

4. **Adaptive algorithms:**
   - Systems that automatically adjust their actions based on new data.

## Tasks that AI solves:

- Identifying hidden patterns in data.
- Creating adaptive strategies that account for market changes.
- Optimizing strategy parameters to achieve better results.

## Advantages of using AI:

- **Speed:** Rapid processing of large data volumes.
- **Accuracy:** Elimination of human errors.
- **Adaptability:** Ability to adjust to new market conditions.

Artificial intelligence significantly improves the performance of trading robots, making them more efficient and adaptive to modern market conditions.
