Artificial intelligence significantly expands the capabilities of trading robots by solving tasks that require complex data analysis and decision-making. Here are the key tasks that AI can perform:

## Main tasks:

1. **Price forecasting:**
   - Identifying trends and patterns in asset prices.
   - Building models for short-term and long-term forecasts.

2. **Market data analysis:**
   - Processing historical data to identify recurring patterns.
   - Analyzing order books and volumes to assess market liquidity.

3. **Signal recognition:**
   - Automatically determining the right moments to enter and exit trades.
   - Using technical indicators in combination with AI.

4. **News and event processing:**
   - Analyzing textual data such as news and social media publications.
   - Assessing the probable impact of events on market movements.

5. **Strategy parameter optimization:**
   - Finding optimal parameter values to improve strategy effectiveness.

AI allows trading robots to act more precisely, predictably, and adapt to constantly changing market conditions.
