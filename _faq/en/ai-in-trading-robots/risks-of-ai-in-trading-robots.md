Using artificial intelligence in trading robots has many advantages but also comes with certain risks. Understanding these risks helps minimize potential losses.

## Main risks:

1. **Overfitting:**
   - The model may fit too closely to historical data, losing the ability to generalize.

2. **Insufficient data:**
   - If data is insufficient or of poor quality, the algorithm may make incorrect decisions.

3. **Technical failures:**
   - High model complexity can lead to errors or delays in operation.

4. **Unpredictable behavior:**
   - In extreme market conditions, AI may make decisions that are difficult to explain.

5. **Ethical and regulatory issues:**
   - Some markets may restrict the use of AI or impose strict requirements.

Using AI requires thorough testing and constant monitoring to avoid unforeseen risks.
