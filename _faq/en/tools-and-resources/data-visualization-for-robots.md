Data visualization is an important stage of strategy analysis that helps better understand the trading robot's behavior and identify patterns.

## Visualization tools:

1. **[TradingView](https://www.tradingview.com/):**
   - A wide range of tools for building charts and analyzing market data.
   - Suitable for visualizing strategy results.

2. **Python with Matplotlib or Plotly:**
   - For more flexible visualization and creating custom charts.
   - Suitable for analyzing large volumes of data.

3. **Excel or Google Sheets:**
   - A simple solution for building diagrams based on trade data.

## Visualization tips:

- Use profitability charts to assess strategy stability.
- Build heat maps to analyze the effectiveness of various parameters.
- Regularly update visualizations to account for current results.

## Recommendations:

- Start with the built-in tools of the platform you are using.
- For complex tasks, try Python or specialized libraries.
- Visualize not only profits but also risks to make more informed decisions.
