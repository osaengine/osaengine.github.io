For a trading robot to work successfully, it is important to regularly analyze its results and maintain detailed trade records. This helps optimize strategies and minimize risks.

## Main aspects of record-keeping:

1. **Recording trades:**
   - Record each trade with the time, instrument, volume, and result.
   - Keep track of profits and losses.

2. **Effectiveness analysis:**
   - Evaluate key indicators such as returns, drawdown, and the ratio of winning to losing trades.
   - Compare current results with previous periods.

3. **Strategy monitoring:**
   - Identify weaknesses in strategies and correct them.
   - Account for changes in market conditions.

## Record-keeping tools:

- **Excel or Google Sheets:** Simple solutions for maintaining a trade journal.

## Tips:

- Regularly update records and check them for accuracy.
- Analyze not only profitability but also strategy stability.
- Use charts and reports to visualize results.
