A trading robot is software that automatically executes trading operations on financial markets according to a predefined algorithm. Such algorithms are developed to achieve specific trading goals, including profit maximization, risk minimization, or optimization of trading processes.

## Key characteristics:

- Automated trade execution
- Elimination of the emotional factor
- Ability to operate 24/7
- High speed of decision-making and order execution

## How does it work?

1. **Strategy development**: Entry and exit rules are defined.
2. **Algorithm programming**: The strategy is implemented in code or using a visual designer.
3. **Historical data testing**: The algorithm's effectiveness is verified.
4. **Live trading launch**: Connection to a broker and working with real data.

## Advantages:

- Elimination of the emotional factor
- High speed of trade execution
- Ability to operate on multiple markets simultaneously
- Increased accuracy of trading strategy execution
