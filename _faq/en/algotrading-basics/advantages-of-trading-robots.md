Trading robots significantly simplify the trading process and offer a number of advantages over manual trading, making it more efficient and convenient.

## Key advantages:

1. **Execution speed:**
   - Robots can instantly react to market changes.
   - The delay between signal and trade execution is reduced.

2. **Absence of emotions:**
   - Psychological errors such as fear or greed are eliminated.
   - All decisions are made strictly according to the algorithm.

3. **Round-the-clock operation:**
   - Robots can work 24/7, processing signals and executing trades at any time.

4. **Multitasking:**
   - Ability to simultaneously analyze multiple markets and instruments.

5. **Increased accuracy:**
   - Errors related to the human factor, such as incorrect data entry, are eliminated.
